Ship Specifications

Dimensions

  • Length (Overall): 199.5 ft
  • Length (Waterline): 184 ft
  • Beam (Main Deck): 41 ft
  • Beam (Overall): 50 ft
  • Design Draft (Amidships): 12.5 ft
  • Freeboard (Amidships): 6.5 ft
  • Air Draft: 88.5 ft
  • Gross Tonnage (ITC): 1,549
  • Displacement (Full Load): 1,842 LT

Performance

  • Cruise Speed: 12 knots
  • Max Speed: 12.5 knots
  • Range: 5,400 nm @ 12 knots
  • Endurance: 21 days (minimum)
  • Fuel Capacity: 50,700 gallons
  • Fuel Consumption: 1,585 gal/day @ 10 knots, 990 gal/day @ 7 knots
  • Main Propulsion: 1,740 hp
  • Bollard Pull: 18,410 lb
  • Ice Class: ABS C0

Laboratory & Science Spaces

  • Main Lab: 520 ft²
  • Wet Lab: 440 ft²
  • Additional Labs: 465 ft²
  • Library/Conference Room/Lounge: 195 ft²
  • Science Storage: 165 ft²
  • Aft Deck Areas: 2,220 ft²
  • 20ft Van Locations: 2

Communication & Crew

  • Satellite Communications: Dual Ku/Ka band
  • Dedicated Data Center: Yes
  • Science Berths: 16
  • Mobility Accessible Berths: 1
  • Crew: 13
  • Total Berths (without Berthing Van): 29

Deck & Handling Equipment

  • A-Frame Dimensions: 25' H x 20' W
  • A-Frame Luffing Load: 15,000 lbf
  • Aft Crane Reach: ~55 ft
  • Aft Crane Lift Rating: 15,000 lbs (static), 7,500 lbs (at sea)
  • Portable A-Frame: 16,000 lbf (NBL down), 6,000 SWT (towing)
  • Portable Crane: 2,200 lb (static) at 25 ft
  • Permanent Winches: 2

Science Equipment

  • Main Mast Instruments: Wave radar, wind speed/direction
  • Met Tower Instruments: Wind speed/direction, barometer, temperature, radiation
  • Multibeam Sonar: EM304, EM2040
  • ADCPs: 75 kHz, 300 kHz
  • Retractable Centerboard: Yes
  • Transducer Pole: Portable, over-the-side
  • Fume Hoods: 2
